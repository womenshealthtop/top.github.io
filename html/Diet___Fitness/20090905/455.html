What You Need To Know About the Schwinn 201 Recumbent Exerci
Source: 未知 Author: net Published date: 2009-09-05

The Schwinn 201 recumbent exercise bike is an older model that's still available sometimes. It's been a big seller so you can find much owner comment on the Internet. The newer models have more features, but the basics are all there on the 201. Those basics are what have made it popular. Here's what to look for if you consider this bike.

Heavy Construction

I've been riding the same Schwinn exercise bike for almost twenty years and I bought it used. That's the reputation of Schwinn bikes. They're known for durability at a moderate price. That's what you get here. You get a machine that won't come apart with hard use. That's exactly what happens with the cheap bikes. Read them hard and they self destruct. Smooth Riding

A quiet, smooth ride makes it easier to keep at that riding. The 201 has a solid electronic resistance system that's whisper quiet and smooth too. That way you can read or watch videos or listen to music which makes the exercise time pass faster. That's the key to fitness using an exercise machine, keeping at it for the long term. Don't forget that big, comfortable recumbent seat too. Schwinn is known for good seats. 织梦内容管理系统

Programming

dedecms.com

Another way to fight boredom is with programmed workouts. An automatic program varies the resistance to pedaling much like you would feel on a real ride. That keeps a ride interesting, not boring. The 201 includes 8 resistance levels plus 6 programs. This doesn't match The newer Schwinn 231, but it's a good, solid package.

copyright dedecms

The Schwinn 201 Recumbent Exercise Bike is a solid, older model with durability and a smooth ride. It doesn't quite match the features of more modern models like the Schwinn 231, but it's a solid choice. You may be able to find a good deal on one too.
