How to Lose 26 Pounds in 7 Weeks

The Fat Burning Furnace program is aimed to help people lose body fat exercising just 15 minutes, 2-3 times per week. It was created by Rob Poulos, who had years of experience testing out all major weight loss methods and finally discovered the efficiency of "short burst" workouts. What are short-burst workouts? They are the ultimate alternative to cardio workouts. Each short burst session is only 15 minutes in duration but are very intense and target all body muscles. One short-burst sessions is equivalent to 90 minutes of cardio, making it the most time-efficient exercise you can do. They differ from cardio in that they burn carbohydrates instead of calories, which make it harder for your body to store body fat. Even though many of us want to lose weight, most of us don't really have the time to exercise for hours every week. The Fat Burning Furnace is great for those with busy schedules who want to see fast results with little effort.
