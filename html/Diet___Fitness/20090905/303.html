A Guide To the Oral HCG Diet

The oral HCG diet has several different components to it that all work together to make the diet a successful way to lose weight. The diet consists of taking small doses of the hormone HCG along with a very specific diet that must be followed for maximum weight loss.

The first thing that you should understand about the oral HCG diet is what exactly HCG is and how it works. HCG, which stands for Human Chorio Gonadotropin, is a naturally made hormone that is found in humans. It is perfectly safe as it is made by pregnant women to help them protect the unborn baby. dedecms.com

The way that the oral HCG diet works is that the hormone actually stimulates the body to take the stored, abnormal fat that a person has and use it as energy. This is what burns fat and calories at a quick pace. Your body is constantly using the stored fat to do anything that it needs energy for, even sleeping or sitting on the couch. On the oral HCG diet, a person takes a small dose of the hormone everyday to constantly keep the body burning the fat. It also helps to make the hypothalamus work efficiently and will raise a person's metabolism.

The reason why it is only recommended to eat 500 calories is because the body is using so much energy from your stored fat that you don't need to consume any more than 500 calories of food in a day. Another factor of the oral HCG diet is you have to eat organic food. The reason why people are overweight to begin with is because of all the chemicals and preservatives found in normal food. By getting rid of those chemicals, your body gets back to its original balance and your metabolism can get set back to normal. When you start adding those chemicals back into your body, you will notice that your cravings as well as your ability to overeat will come back.
