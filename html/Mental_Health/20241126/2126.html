Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is not just the absence of illness; it’s the foundation for overall health and happiness. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), nearly 1 in 8 people globally live with a mental disorder. Despite this, access to resources and societal understanding remains limited in many areas.
